# Template: Generic Team Article Issue

Use this template when creating "generate article" issues for NFL teams.

**Issues must be GENERIC — no pre-written angle.** Idea generation is Step 1 of the pipeline.

## Issue Title Format

```
Article: {ABBR} - {Full Team Name} — 2026 Offseason
```

## Issue Body Format

```markdown
## Working Title
{ABBR} - {Full Team Name} — 2026 Offseason First Post

## Angle / Tension
**IDEA GENERATION REQUIRED:** Do not use a pre-written angle.

As the first pipeline step, research this team's 2026 offseason situation and identify
the single most pressing/interesting question facing this franchise RIGHT NOW.

Use current data: 2025 season results, 2026 cap situation, coaching staff, draft position.
DO NOT rely on training data alone — fetch current info before generating the angle.

## Primary Team
{ABBR}

## Depth Level
2 — The Beat (balanced data + story, 2000–3500 words, 3–4 agents)

## Suggested Agents
Cap + relevant scheme expert + team expert. Lead selects based on generated angle.

## Target Publish Date
ASAP — 2026 offseason
```

**Labels:** `squad`, `article`
