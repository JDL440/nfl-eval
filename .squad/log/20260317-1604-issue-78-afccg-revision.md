## Pattern Learned

**Disclaim → Anchor → Reframe:** When an article uses a game as its narrative hook but the analytical argument is about a different timeframe, (1) disclaim the hook as evidence, (2) anchor to the correct timeframe, (3) reframe the closing to match. Applied successfully here: AFCCG = emotional hook, regular season = evidentiary base.
