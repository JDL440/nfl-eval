### Key Storylines to Monitor

1. **QB Decision at #2 Overall** — This is THE defining decision of the offseason. Ward vs Sanders vs trade up vs BPA defender. GM Mougey's first major draft decision will shape the franchise for a decade.

2. **$91M Dead Money Hangover** — The ghosts of Rodgers ($35M), Sauce ($11M), Quinnen ($9.8M), and Mosley ($7.65M) are eating the 2026 cap. But this pain is temporary — by 2027 the Jets will have $167M in cap space (2nd in NFL).

3. **Aaron Glenn Year 2** — Overhauled 12 of 22 coaches. Added Frank Reich (OC), Bill Musgrave (QB), Karl Dunbar (DL). This is essentially a new coaching staff with a 2nd-year HC. Must show improvement or seat gets warm.

4. **Garrett Wilson Utilization** — 395 receiving yards for a #10 overall pick in his 4th year is criminal. Wilson's talent isn't the issue — QB play is. If Jets get a competent QB, Wilson should explode.

5. **T'Vondre Sweat Value** — Acquiring a 25-year-old, 366-lb nose tackle on a $1.7M cap hit for a declining Jermaine Johnson is outstanding value. Sweat could anchor this DL for years.

6. **Fashanu + Membou Bookend Tackles** — Ages 23 and 21. Combined cap hit of $12.8M on rookie deals. This is an elite young OT pair. Protect them at all costs.

7. **NE's Dominance** — Patriots took our AVT, made the Super Bowl, have Drake Maye. The gap between NYJ and NE is enormous. Closing it requires hitting on the 2026 draft.

### Learnings

- ✅ Jets have elite draft capital (two R1s, two R2s in 2026, plus 2027 R1 from IND) but massive dead money ($91M, 2nd worst in NFL)
- ✅ The 2026 cap pain is temporary — 2027 projected space of $167M gives massive flexibility for future FA classes
- ✅ QB is the existential need. No amount of defensive spending fixes 140 passing yards/game (32nd)
- ✅ OT pair of Fashanu/Membou is a legitimate franchise-building foundation
- ✅ Division rival NE is the clear threat — Maye/Vrabel combo is the AFC East's dominant force for the foreseeable future
- ✅ MIA's implosion (Tua release, $99M dead cap) means the division's bottom is even worse than the Jets' situation
- ✅ Glenn's coaching overhaul (12 of 22 coaches replaced) signals organizational awareness that 2025 was a scheme/coaching problem, not just a talent problem
