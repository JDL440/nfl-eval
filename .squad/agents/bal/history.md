## Key Takeaways & Strategic Analysis
_Last updated: 2026-03-12_

### Strengths 💪
- ✅ **Elite defensive talent:** Hamilton, Roquan, Madubuike (when healthy), Humphrey, Wiggins, Starks, Hendrickson
- ✅ **Lamar Jackson:** MVP-caliber QB on manageable 2026 cap hit ($34.5M)
- ✅ **Cap flexibility:** 2nd-3rd most cap space in NFL. Can still make moves
- ✅ **Young secondary:** Wiggins (22), Starks (22), Tampa (23), Green (22) — core for years
- ✅ **Hendrickson signing:** 4yr/$112M for elite pass rusher, stolen from division rival

### Weaknesses ⚠️
- 🔴 **Center catastrophe:** Linderbaum to LV is the biggest loss. Bullock is a dramatic downgrade
- 🔴 **TE wasteland:** Lost Likely AND Kolar. Andrews at 30 is the only TE. Can't run 12/13 personnel
- 🔴 **No 1st-round pick:** Can't replace premium talent via draft efficiently
- 🔴 **No punter:** Literally need to find one
- 🟡 **Aging skill positions:** Henry (32), Andrews (30), Stanley (31), Humphrey (29), Awuzie (30)
- 🟡 **IR concerns:** Madubuike, Washington, Buchanan, Hill all on IR/injured at season's end
- 🟡 **8-9 season:** Culture/confidence questions after disappointing year

2. **Re-sign Tyler Huntley** — Need a backup QB who knows the system
3. **Explore FA tight end market** — Durham Smythe? Veteran TE available?
4. **Draft CENTER at #45** — Must replace Linderbaum
