## Key Storylines to Monitor

1. **Jaxson Dart development** — Can the 2nd-year QB take a leap under Matt Nagy? His rookie tape showed flashes (283 yds vs DEN, 246 vs WSH) but inconsistency (33 yds vs MIN).
2. **Cam Skattebo's role** — Rookie was explosive (98 rush yds vs PHI, 79 vs LAC). Questionable designation — monitor health.
3. **Andrew Thomas health** — Franchise LT has Q designation. If he goes down, Mbow (rookie) is next.
4. **Burns + Carter EDGE duo** — Combined $47M cap hit but could be elite. Carter's rookie year in a 3-4 scheme is key.
5. **Cap crunch** — $2M effective space with ~$11M draft pool needed. Restructures imminent.
6. **Draft #5 pick** — WR (Carnell Tate?) or trade down? Defines the 2026 trajectory.
7. **Ravens pipeline** — How many more BAL players follow Harbaugh? Culture import.
8. **Wan'Dale Robinson replacement** — Slot WR void is real. Draft or trade needed.
