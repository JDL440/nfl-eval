## Learnings & Notes

1. ✅ Ben Johnson hire from DET was transformational — 5-12 to 11-6 in Year 1.
2. ✅ DJ Moore trade was cap-motivated: freed $16.5M, netted 2nd-round pick. Bears betting on Odunze + Burden as WR1/2.
3. ✅ Dennis Allen's defensive scheme demands man-coverage DBs and speed at LB — free agency signings (Bryant, Bush) align perfectly.
4. 📊 Safety is the #1 roster crisis. Lost three safeties (Brisker, CJGJ, Byard) with only Coby Bryant as the established starter.
5. 📊 Cap is TIGHT ($2.4-6.8M). Draft pool (~$5.2M) may require restructures to Sweat or Jackson.
6. 📊 Two 2nd-round picks give Bears flexibility to trade up in R1 or address two needs simultaneously.
7. ⚠️ Maxx Crosby rumors are the splashiest storyline but cap constraints make a deal very difficult without corresponding moves.
8. 📊 Division is brutal: DET is loaded, GB added Micah Parsons, MIN adding defensive veterans. Bears must nail 2026 draft.
