### Strategic Assessment
_Last updated: 2026-03-12_

**Window Status:** ✅ **OPEN — Contender.** 14-3 record, AFCCG appearance, young QB on rookie deal. Window is NOW.

**Offseason Philosophy:** "Run it back" — retain core, fill gaps through draft. Conservative FA approach with short-term deals. Meinerz restructure created flexibility.

**Key Strengths:**
- 📊 Elite pass rush (Cooper/Bonitto/Elliss/Allen)
- 📊 Franchise CB (Surtain II — top-5 at position)
- 📊 Young franchise QB on cheap deal (Nix through 2027 at $5M/yr)
- 📊 Strong OL interior (Meinerz/Powers)
- 📊 Clean dead money ($1.2M — 30th lowest in NFL)
- 📊 Coaching stability under Payton

**Key Risks:**
- ❓ Bo Nix ankle recovery — if he's not 100%, offense could struggle
- ❓ Aging OT duo (Bolles 33, McGlinchey 31) — need succession plan
- ❓ TE position is weak for a Payton offense that traditionally relies heavily on TE
- ❓ Lost DL depth (JFM departure) without replacement yet
- ❓ Cap getting tighter as veteran contracts escalate into 2027
- ❓ Davis Webb as play-caller is unproven — first-year OC calling plays

**Critical 2026 Decisions:**
1. Draft ILB or S at #30? CJ Allen is the consensus pick
2. Address TE in R2 — Eli Stowers fits the profile
3. Plan OT succession — Bolles/McGlinchey both on expensive declining deals
4. Monitor Nix recovery — if healthy, extend before price skyrockets
5. Watch McGlinchey as potential cap casualty in 2027 ($23.8M hit)
