### Strategic Outlook
_Last updated: 2026-03-12_

**Window Status:** 📊 OPEN but narrowing. Hurts (27), Brown (28), Smith (27), Barkley (29), Mailata (28) are in their primes. Lane Johnson (35) has 1-2 years left. The 2027 cap explosion ($103M+) gives runway to reload — but the offense's window depends on these core players staying healthy and productive.

**Critical Decisions Ahead:**
1. **Jalen Carter: Extend or Trade?** — This shapes the entire offseason strategy
2. **EDGE at #23 in the draft?** — Must find a pass rusher (first or second round)
3. **TE solution** — Draft, sign, or trade for a receiving TE to replace Goedert's production
4. **Restructures** — Hurts/Brown restructures could create $30M+ in 2026 space for signings
5. **Punter** — Small but must-fill roster need

**Biggest Concerns:**
- 🚨 No proven edge rusher on the roster
- 🚨 $52M dead money draining 2026 resources
- 🚨 TE room went from top-5 (Goedert) to bottom-10 overnight
- 🚨 Washington emerging as a legitimate division contender with Daniels + Oweh
