1. **Dead money is killing flexibility.** $68.4M in dead cap (5th worst in NFL) from Howard trade, Kirk release, Mason/CGJ/Autry departures. This limits FA spending despite reasonable active roster costs.
2. **The Stroud/Anderson extension cliff is coming.** Both are on rookie deals through 2026. Combined they cost ~$22.7M now but will cost $85-95M/yr after extensions. Houston must build the roster cheaply everywhere else.
3. **JAX is the biggest division threat.** Won the division, added Travis Hunter. Texans need to close the gap.
4. **TEN is rebuilding aggressively.** Saleh + Daboll + Bradley coaching staff with Cam Ward at QB. Could be competitive by 2027.
5. **OL was the biggest weakness in 2025.** Howard trade left a hole. Smith (RT) and Ingram (RG) extensions address it. Ersery at LT is the long-term answer. Need depth.
6. **Interior DL is the most critical draft need.** Lost Settle, Fatukasi, Hinish. Rankins (31) can't carry the load. Must address in R1-R2.
