## Learnings

- **web_search is the most effective tool for league-wide sweeps** — aggregates across ESPN, NFL.com, CBS, FOX, PFF, Sporting News, Yahoo in one call. Use for initial sweeps, then web_fetch for deep dives.
- **2026 offseason is historically active** — 12+ new HCs, Mahomes ACL, Tua release with record dead cap, Parsons trade, Crosby trade voided. More roster churn than typical year.
- **Failed physicals can void blockbuster trades** — Crosby/BAL deal shows medical risk is real even at the highest level.
- **Defense-heavy draft class** reshapes trade market — teams needing OL may overpay to trade up since EDGE/LB/S dominate top picks.
- **MIA's Tua dead cap ($67.4M in 2026 + $31.8M in 2027)** is the largest in NFL history.
- **Multiple teams with 2+ first-round picks** (NYJ, DAL via trade) creates unusual draft-night trade-up leverage.
- **PIT's offseason strategy** — Building massive win-now roster (Metcalf trade, Pittman trade, Dean, Brisker, Dowdle) around potential Rodgers return. All-in gamble.
