## Learnings

- **web_search is the most effective tool for league-wide sweeps** — aggregates across ESPN, NFL.com, CBS, FOX, PFF, Sporting News, Yahoo in one call. Use for initial sweeps, then web_fetch for specific team/player deep dives.
- **2026 offseason is historically active** — 12+ new head coaches, Mahomes ACL, Tua release with record dead cap, Parsons trade. More roster churn than typical year.
- **Defense-heavy draft class** reshapes trade market — teams needing OL may overpay to trade up since EDGE/LB/S dominate top picks.
- **MIA's Tua dead cap ($67.4M in 2026)** is the largest in NFL history — signals how aggressively teams are willing to eat costs to reset.
- **Multiple teams with 2+ first-round picks** (NYJ, DAL via trade) creates unusual draft-night trade-up leverage.
