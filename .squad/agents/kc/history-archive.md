## 2025 Season Review
_Last updated: 2026-03-12 via espn.com schedule_

- ✅ **Record:** 6-11 — Chiefs missed playoffs for first time since 2017
- ✅ **Started 0-2** (L vs LAC 27-21, L vs PHI 20-17), rallied to 5-3, then collapsed down the stretch
- ✅ **Patrick Mahomes torn ACL/LCL** Week 15 vs LAC (Dec 14, 2025) — season-ending
- ✅ Chris Oladokun and Shane Buechele started final 3 games (Weeks 16-18)
- ✅ Lost 6 of final 7 games after Mahomes injury
- 📊 Offense struggled all year — ranked bottom half in rushing, lacked explosive WR play
- 📊 Defense kept games close but couldn't carry the load alone
- ✅ Division results: Split with DEN (1-1), split with LAC (0-2), swept LV (1-1 — won Wk7 31-0, lost Wk18 14-12)
