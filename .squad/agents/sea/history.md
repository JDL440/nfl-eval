### Scheme Identity
_Last updated: 2026-03-13 via coaching staff analysis_

**Offense:**
- ✅ 2025 alignment: 3WR/1TE base (11 personnel) with heavy play-action
- 📊 2026 projection: Fleury likely introduces more zone-run concepts from SF tree; may increase 12-personnel (2 TE) usage given his TE coaching background
- ✅ Tempo: Fleury describes his offense as "fast and violent and aggressive"
- 📊 JSN as alpha WR1 with Kupp (possession/red zone) and Shaheed (speed/returns) as complementary pieces
- ❓ Red zone approach may shift — Fleury emphasizes dynamic run game in scoring territory

**Defense:**
- ✅ Base 3-4 under Macdonald/Durde — aggressive, multiple-front defense
- ✅ Coverage-heavy approach with versatile DBs (Witherspoon can shadow WR1s, Emmanwori plays big nickel)
- 📊 Pass rush relies on Williams interior pressure + edge rotation (Lawrence/Nwosu), but lost Mafe's speed rush
- ❓ May need to blitz more in 2026 without Mafe's natural pass rush ability
