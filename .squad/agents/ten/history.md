### Organizational Direction
_Last updated: 2026-03-12_

📊 **The Titans are in Year 1 of a reboot with clear strategic pillars:**

1. **Build around Cam Ward** — Daboll hire is specifically to develop the rookie QB. Ward has 4 years of rookie-deal cap control (~$11M/yr). This is the window.
2. **Saleh's defense-first identity** — Cover-3, 4-3 front. Invested heavily in CBs (Taylor $58M, Flott $45M, Sneed $19.6M). Need EDGE to complete the vision.
3. **Cap flexibility is the #1 asset** — $39M effective now, $70M effective in 2027, $188M in 2028. Can be aggressive in trades and signings.
4. **Draft #4 is premium currency** — Can draft a franchise EDGE/WR or trade down for a massive haul. Either path is viable.
5. **Borgonzi's philosophy** — Aggressive talent acquisition (traded for Johnson, Thomas; signed 6+ FAs). Willing to pay market rate for starters.
6. **Daboll + Saleh = proven NFL coaches** — Both have coordinator and HC experience. Credibility matters for player acquisition.
