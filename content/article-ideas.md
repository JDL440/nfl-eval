## Content Strategy Notes

### Publish Schedule
- **Offseason (now through June):** 1×/week, Tuesdays at 10 AM PT
- **Training camp (July-August):** 2×/week, Tues + Thurs at 10 AM PT
- **Regular season:** 2×/week, Tues (recap) + Thurs (preview) at 10 AM PT
- **Breaking news:** publish immediately regardless of schedule

### Growth Levers
- **The "expert panel disagrees" format** is the hook — lean into it for every article
- **Cross-post to Reddit** (r/Seahawks, r/NFL_Draft, r/nfl) with excerpts
- **Article #1 topic should be the meta piece** ("How we built a 46-agent panel") for initial Substack growth
- **Draft week is the highest-traffic window** — publish daily April 23-27
- **The kickoff game** (Sep 10, SEA hosts) is the single biggest content moment — have a massive preview ready

### Quality Gates
- ✅ Every article goes through **Editor** before publish (mandatory since Article 2)
- ✅ Every article uses the **substack-article skill** for consistent structure
- ✅ Every stat, name, and contract figure gets **web-verified** by Editor
- ✅ The RB article (#1) still needs a retroactive Editor review
