### 1. "Three 1,000-yard seasons" should be "four"

- **Line 182:** "proven WR1s with three 1,000-yard seasons and elite speed" → **four**

**Source:** The article's own data table (lines 107–113) and the discussion prompt data anchors.

### 2. Saints historical comp conflates two different eras

**Line 81:** *"In the Saints' dynasty years, Michael Thomas commanded the underneath attention while Ted Ginn Jr. and Devery Henderson got single-coverage deep shots."*

Devery Henderson played for the Saints **2004–2008** (and briefly in 2012). Michael Thomas was drafted in **2016**. They were never on the same roster. Ted Ginn Jr. overlapped with Thomas (2017–2019), but Henderson did not. As written, the sentence implies all three were contemporaries — which is factually wrong.

**Fix options:**
- (a) Drop Henderson entirely: *"...while Ted Ginn Jr. got single-coverage deep shots."*
- (b) Split into two era references: *"In the earlier Brees years, Devery Henderson provided the deep speed; in the Thomas era, Ted Ginn Jr. filled that role."*
- (c) Follow the Offense position paper's framing, which correctly identifies this as a "Colston-Henderson model, updated" — two separate historical references, not one combined roster.
