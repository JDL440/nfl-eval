## Panel Composition Rationale

**Why these 4 agents:**

1. **HOU (Team Expert)** — Required for all team-specific articles. Owns roster context, division dynamics, and the competitive window reality (JAX won the division).

2. **Cap (Salary Cap Expert)** — Core of the story. The article is fundamentally about cap architecture: modeling the mega-extensions, understanding the year-by-year cap crunch, and identifying restructure opportunities. Cap will provide the actual numbers that drive the narrative.

3. **Draft (Draft Expert)** — Houston has 8 picks including 3 in the top 69. This is the last cheap talent infusion before the cap compresses. Draft will map specific prospects (Christen Miller, LT Overton, Peter Woods, Caleb Banks) to Houston's needs (DT, LB, OT depth).

4. **Defense (Defensive Scheme Expert)** — Interior DL is identified as a crisis-level need (lost Settle, Fatukasi, Hinish; Rankins is 31). Defense will explain how these departures change the defensive identity in DeMeco Ryans' 4-3 scheme and which draft DTs are scheme fits vs. athletic mismatches.
