## Rationale
This is a 4-agent panel (appropriate for Depth Level 2). The tension is between offense and defense philosophy, so we need both a scheme expert and a cap expert to argue the tradeoffs. Draft provides the prospect layer. TEN provides the franchise context that ties it all together.

**Intentional friction points:**
- Cap vs. Defense: Cap may argue Ridley cut + WR at #4 maximizes Ward's Year 2; Defense may argue EDGE at #4 is irreplaceable value and the defense carries Ward.
- Draft vs. TEN: Draft evaluates prospects in a vacuum; TEN contextualizes them against organizational needs and power dynamics.
- All agents should be told: *"Disagreement is encouraged. The article is better when panelists push back on each other."*
