## Panel Size Justification

**Depth Level:** 2 — The Beat (3-4 agents allowed)
**Selected:** 4 agents

**Rationale:**
- **DET** is mandatory — owns team context, roster dynamics, and Dan Campbell's window timeline
- **Cap** is mandatory — Hutchinson extension looming ($50M+ AAV), Joseph extension coming, limited spending flexibility needs hard numbers
- **Defense** is critical — EDGE vs. OT decision is fundamentally a defensive scheme question; Branch/Joseph injury timelines affect urgency
- **Draft** is critical — Pick #17 is the fulcrum of the entire article; need prospect-level analysis of both EDGE and OT at this pick

**Why not 3 agents?** The EDGE vs. OT decision requires both scheme analysis (Defense) AND draft board reality (Draft). Collapsing those into one agent would miss either the scheme fit or the availability/value analysis.

**Why not 5 agents?** Offense, Analytics, and Media were considered but rejected:
- **Offense** — Not needed; OT need is binary (Borom is not a starter), no scheme debate
- **Analytics** — Not needed for Level 2; draft value charts can be cited by Draft agent
- **Media** — Not needed; this is not a news-driven article, it's roster construction analysis
