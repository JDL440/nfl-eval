# Panel Composition: Mahomes Is Racing Back — But to What?

## Panel Size: 3 agents (Level 2 — The Beat)

## Selected Panel

### 1. KC (Kansas City Chiefs Expert) — Primary
**Why:** This is a Chiefs article. KC holds the deepest roster knowledge, division rival intelligence, and offseason context. KC will anchor the roster audit and provide the "state of the franchise" perspective.

**Specific angle:** Grade the net talent change across every position group. Compare what Mahomes had in September 2025 vs. what he'll have in September 2026. Provide the honest ceiling/floor projections.

### 2. Cap (Salary Cap Expert)
**Why:** The Chiefs' offseason is fundamentally a cap story. They cleared ~$98M through trades, cuts, and restructures. Jones' $44.85M hit and Mahomes' $85.25M 2027 bomb are the twin financial cliffs. Cap's analysis will determine whether this is a one-year window or sustainable.

**Specific angle:** Map the 2026 cap table, project 2027 exposure, evaluate the Jones contract decision tree (extend/restructure/accept), and quantify the financial cost of the McDuffie trade vs. what it enabled.

### 3. Offense (Offensive Scheme Expert)
**Why:** The most interesting schematic question in the NFL right now: how does Andy Reid rebuild the offense around a post-ACL Mahomes, a new explosive RB (Walker), a potentially absent WR1 (Rice), and a 36-year-old Kelce? The scheme adjustments will define whether this team competes or struggles.

**Specific angle:** Project the 2026 offensive identity — more run-heavy? More screen/YAC based? How does Reid protect Mahomes early in the season? What does the WR rotation look like with/without Rice?

## Agents NOT Included (and why)

| Agent | Reason for Exclusion |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Draft** | Two first-round picks are critical, but the draft hasn't happened yet. Prospect analysis is speculative. Better suited for a follow-up draft preview article. |
| **Defense** | Defensive changes are significant but secondary to the offensive rebuild narrative. Covered adequately by KC's roster audit. |
| **Injury** | Mahomes' ACL timeline is well-documented. KC agent can cover the relevant recovery facts without a full injury deep-dive. |
| **Media** | Rumors (Rice suspension, Tyreek Hill reunion) are captured in KC's history.md. No separate media sweep needed. |

## Expected Disagreements
- **Cap vs. KC:** Cap may argue the 2027 cliff makes this a one-year-only window. KC may argue the draft capital makes it sustainable.
- **Offense vs. KC:** Offense may be skeptical that Walker + Worthy is enough firepower. KC may be more bullish on the Reid scheme factor.
- **All three on Rice:** The suspension wildcard changes everything. Each agent should model both scenarios (Rice plays / Rice suspended).
