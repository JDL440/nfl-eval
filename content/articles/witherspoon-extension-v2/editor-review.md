## 🟡 SUGGESTIONS

### 1. Cap quote (line 122) is a polished synthesis, not verbatim

> *"Devon Witherspoon at $30 million a year keeps the championship window open. At $33 million, you're choosing between your corner and your pass rush — and championship defenses don't make that choice." — Cap*

This quote does not appear in `cap-position.md`. It accurately captures Cap's argument but was crafted by the Writer. Same pattern flagged in JSN review (Third pass). The other five expert quotes in the article are verbatim from position files.

**Action:** Consider converting to narrative summary (drop quote marks) or adding an editorial note that quotes are lightly condensed. Not a publication blocker, but a credibility risk if source material is ever published alongside the article.

### 2. "Roughly one-fifth of the cap" slightly overstates (line 120)

The article says combined Witherspoon-at-$33M + JSN-at-$34M spending would be "roughly one-fifth of the cap by 2029." Against the projected 2029 cap of $376M, $68–70M is ~18.1–18.6% — closer to one-sixth than one-fifth. The word "roughly" provides cover, and this is Cap's own language from the position file, but cap articles live on precision.

**Action:** Consider "approaching one-fifth" or "nearly 19%" for tighter accuracy. Minor item.

### 3. Woolen and Bryant contract details unsourced in v2 material (line 29)

> "Riq Woolen left for Philadelphia on a deal worth $15 million per year. Coby Bryant went to Chicago for 3 years and $40 million."

These destination teams and contract figures do not appear in any v2 position file or the discussion prompt. They are consistent with v1 project data and aren't contradicted, but if v2 is treated as self-contained, these should be verified against the current project universe.

**Action:** Confirm against SEA agent's history.md or soften to non-specific language. Low risk — details appear stable across versions.

### 4. Image placeholders still pending

Two inline image placeholders exist (lines 62, 199) with `Placeholder for generated art` labels. No images have been generated yet (`content/images/witherspoon-extension-v2/` does not exist). Article needs image generation (Phase 4b) before publisher pass.
