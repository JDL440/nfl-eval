## Key Decisions & Outcomes

### Primary Outcome: Article Consensus on JSN Extension Price

**Panel Convergence:** $31–33M AAV range
- Cap: $34M (cap hit + flexibility math)
- PlayerRep: $36M (player leverage + market tier)
- SEA: $31–32M (competitive without breaking cap)
- Offense: $28M (elite with budget flexibility)

**Synthesis:** The range threads all positions. JSN is elite-tier WR (Jefferson / Hill / Lamb level), but Seattle's cap constraints and secondary needs (Bryant loss) require smart structuring, not elite pricing.

### Secondary Findings

1. **WA Tax (SB 6346) is a front-loading accelerant**
   - Effective 2028-01-01: 9.9% tax on income >$1M/yr
   - Signing bonus paid in 2026 = pre-tax income (no state tax)
   - Creates time-sensitive window strengthening the "extend now" case
   - Cap + PlayerRep identified this as critical article angle

2. **Shaheed Signal**
   - PlayerRep non-obvious insight: $51M Shaheed re-sign is JSN's best negotiating weapon
   - Seattle tipped its hand: can't claim cap crisis after paying WR2 $17M/yr
   - Article tension: confidence in WR investment vs. ability to pay extension

3. **Tag War Unanimous Rejection**
   - All panelists agreed: franchise tag is off the table (injury risk + salary cap inefficiency)
   - Clear headline consensus
