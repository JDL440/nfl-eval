1. **✅ Full Rebuild Mode:** The Raiders are executing a complete tear-down and rebuild under GM John Spytek and HC Klint Kubiak. Traded franchise player Crosby for draft capital. Cleared dead money. Signed foundational pieces (Linderbaum, Dean, Walker, Paye).

2. **✅ Franchise QB Incoming:** With the #1 pick and Mendoza as consensus selection, the Raiders are building the roster to support a rookie QB — elite OL (Linderbaum + Miller), weapons (Bowers, Jeanty, Nailor), and a rebuilt defense.

3. **📊 Cap Flexibility:** Despite $52M+ dead money in 2026, the Raiders have $37-45M in cap space NOW and project to have $135M+ in 2027. They can be aggressive in both short and long-term building.

4. **📊 Draft Capital Advantage:** Two R1 picks (#1, #14), ~11 total picks. This is generational draft capital. Can draft QB + premium defender/weapon, or trade #14 for even more picks.

5. **📊 Division Window:** KC's secondary is weakened (lost McDuffie, Watson, Cook). LAC lost Oweh. DEN has QB uncertainty. The AFC West gap is narrowing. LV won't contend in 2026 but the rebuild timeline aligns with rivals potentially declining.
