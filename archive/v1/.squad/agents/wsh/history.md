### Key Observations & Strategic Notes
_Last updated: 2026-03-12_

1. **📊 Cap Window is NOW:** With Daniels on his rookie deal ($10.3M cap hit, ~$22M dead = untouchable) and $66M+ in space, the Commanders have a 2-3 year window to build a contender before Daniels' extension kicks in. The 2027 projections ($164M space) give even more flexibility.

2. **📊 Defensive Spending is Real:** Oweh ($100M), Payne ($28M), Kinlaw ($16.2M), Armstrong ($12M), Chaisson ($11M), Luvu ($11.3M), Chenal ($4.3M), Settle ($24M total) — the front office is building the defense as the roster's backbone.

3. **📊 Offense Needs Help Badly:** RB has no starter, WR2 is vacant, C is a gap. The offense around Daniels is incomplete. The #7 pick and remaining cap space MUST address this.

4. **📊 R1 #7 Decision is Franchise-Defining:** With no R2 or R4 picks, R1 must hit. Top candidates: RB Jeremiyah Love, WR prospect, or trade-down for more picks to fill multiple holes.

5. **❓ Coordinator Risk:** Two first-time coordinators (Blough, Jones) is historically risky. If the offense stalls, Quinn's seat could warm despite the roster talent investment.

6. **📊 NFC East is Shifting:** PHI lost significant defensive pieces (Phillips, Dean, Blankenship, Huff retired). DAL is loading up defensively. NYG is rebuilding. The division is more open than it's been in years.

7. **⚠️ KICKER ALERT:** No kicker on the roster. Must sign one before training camp. This is a gap that needs immediate attention.
