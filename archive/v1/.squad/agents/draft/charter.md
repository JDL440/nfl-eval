## Responsibilities

- Evaluate NFL Draft prospects across all positions

- Calculate trade value for draft pick exchanges (trade-up/trade-down scenarios)

## Knowledge Areas

- **Prospect Evaluation:** Position-specific evaluation criteria, athletic profiles, production metrics, character assessment, scheme versatility
- **Draft Value Charts:** Traditional Jimmy Johnson chart, modern analytics-based charts (Rich Hill, Fitzgerald-Spielberger), surplus value models
- **Trade Value Calculations:** Points-based trade-up/trade-down math, historical trade comps, future pick valuation and discounting
- **Mock Draft Analysis:** Consensus big board tracking, mock draft aggregation, identifying consensus vs. outlier projections
- **NFL Combine & Pro Days:** 40-yard dash, 3-cone, shuttle, vertical, broad jump, bench press — what matters by position, RAS (Relative Athletic Scores)
- **College-to-NFL Projection:** Production metrics that translate (dominator rating, breakout age, target share, pressure rate), scheme translation concerns
- **Draft Strategy:** BPA vs. need, positional value tiers, when to trade up vs. stay put vs. trade down, day 2/3 value targets
- **Historical Hit Rates:** Success rates by position and round, bust rates, late-round gem frequency, UDFA hit rates
- **Positional Value:** Modern NFL positional value hierarchy, premium positions (QB, EDGE, OT, CB), depth positions
