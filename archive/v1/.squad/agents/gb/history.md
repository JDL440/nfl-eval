### Strategic Assessment
_Last updated: 2026-03-12_

**Window Status:** 📊 **ALL-IN** — The Parsons trade (two 1st-rounders + Clark) signals Gutekunst is going for it NOW with Love's prime.

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Elite pass rush upside (Parsons + LVN + Brooks when healthy)
- ✅ Strong OL core (Tom, Banks, Rhyan all extended)
- ✅ Young, cost-controlled skill talent (Reed, Golden, Cooper, Bullard)
- ✅ 2027 cap outlook excellent ($84.6M projected)
- ✅ Gannon hire upgrades defensive coaching significantly

**Weaknesses:**
- ⚠️ Parsons ACL — if he misses significant time, EDGE rush craters
- ⚠️ Cornerback is a glaring hole — no true CB1 on roster
- ⚠️ No 1st-round picks in 2026 OR 2027 — limited ability to restock via draft
- ⚠️ $39.2M dead money from Gary/Clark trades constraining 2026 cap
- ⚠️ WR depth thin after Doubs departure
- ⚠️ Kicker remains unresolved

**Offseason Grade So Far:** 📊 **B** — Hargrave, Franklin, St-Juste, Rhyan extension are solid moves. But CB and EDGE depth still need addressing before draft. The championship window math only works if Parsons is healthy.
