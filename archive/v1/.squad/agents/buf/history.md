### Learnings
_Accumulated from research sessions_

1. **Bills are cap-strapped in 2026-2027** — Josh Allen's mega-deal ($44.2M→$56.1M→$62.4M) dominates. Beane must continue restructuring to compete. Relief comes in 2028.
2. **Scheme change matters** — From McDermott's 4-3 to Leonhard's 3-4. Players like Rousseau (now SLB edge), Oliver (now LDE), and Walker (NT) may see role changes. Watch for fit issues.
3. **MIA is not a 2026 threat** — Dolphins blew up their roster (Tua, Hill, Chubb all gone). Bills benefit directly (signed Chubb).
4. **NE is the rising rival** — Patriots spending aggressively around Drake Maye. Doubs, Vera-Tucker, Jones are quality adds. Watch for 2027 as their competitive window opens.
5. **NYJ's dead money is astronomical** — $91.2M dead cap, 2nd most in NFL. They have talent but are hamstrung financially.
6. **D.J. Moore trade cost R2 pick** — Bills now lack a Day 2 pick, making R1 (#26) and R3 (#91) critical selections. CB or OT likely at 26.
7. **Dead money problem** — $46.4M in dead money (9th most) from Milano, Johnson, Bosa, McGovern (old deal), Jones, Samuel, Epenesa, Ogunjobi departures.
8. **Bradley Chubb injury flag** — Signed 3yr/$43.5M but has ACL/knee history from MIA. Bills need EDGE depth as insurance.
9. **Deone Walker is the DL future** — 2025 R1 pick, 6'7" 331 lbs, starting at NT in the 3-4. Massive upside.
10. **Joe Brady's "Super Bowl or bust" mandate** — Ownership fired McDermott after 12-5 and a Divisional loss. Expectation is clear: Super Bowl or else.
