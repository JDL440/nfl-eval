## Team Needs
_Last updated: 2026-03-12 — synthesized from depth chart, cap data, FA moves, and media analysis_

### Priority 1 — Must Address
1. **📊 WR2/WR3** — Behind Olave, the WR room is thin. Vele is a slot/size guy, Polk is a project, Palmer is depth. Need a true WR2 who can win outside. **Draft R1 or R2 target.**
2. **📊 CB2** — Lost Alontae Taylor (3yr/$58M to TEN). Quincy Riley is unproven. McKinstry is CB1, but opposite corner is a massive hole. **Could be R1 target at #8.**
3. **📊 EDGE depth** — Granderson ($18M) and Chase Young ($9.6M) are the starters, but there's no proven 3rd rusher. Cameron Jordan is 36 and a UFA. **R2-R3 target.**

### Priority 2 — Important
4. **📊 DT/NT** — Godchaux is 32, Shepherd is 33. Bresee is the future but needs a partner. **Mid-round depth needed.**
5. **📊 LB depth** — Elliss and Werner are starters, but Stutsman is a raw rookie. Depth beyond top 3 is thin. **R4+ target.**
6. **📊 WR depth/speed** — No true deep threat after Shaheed was traded to SEA. Palmer has speed but hasn't produced.

### Priority 3 — Monitor
7. **❓ Safety** — Reid is 29, Blackmon is questionable. Sanker is a developing rookie. Could address in mid-rounds.
8. **❓ OL depth** — Starters are solid (Banks, Edwards, McCoy, Ruiz, Fuaga) but backup quality behind them is thin.
9. **❓ TE2** — Fant was signed but is 28 and declining. Matavao is a developmental project.
